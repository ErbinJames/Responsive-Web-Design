Top 3 tourist Spots in La Union

Urbiztondo Surfing Site

Urbiztondo Surfing Area, the surfing mecca of Northern Philippines,offers ideal waves for beginners and intermediate surfers. It hosts international competitions, including World Surfing League events and the 2019 SEA Games.

Tangadan Falls Adventure

Nestled in the mountains of San Gabriel, La Union, Tangadan Falls is a breathtaking natural masterpiece. Despite modern touches, its serene ambiance continues to calm senses, cleanse souls, and showcase the beauty of nature.

Immuki Island

Immuki Island, once called Kaparingitan, got its name from the sea slugs (Babao) that thrive on dead corals, with its main lagoon resembling a unique shape. Today, it is a stunning destination known for its mangroves, crystal-clear waters, and diverse marine life.
